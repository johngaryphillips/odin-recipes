Hot Dog

Description

Hot dogs are an american pasttime meal. It consists of a long cylinder bun with a link sausage in the center

Ingredients

  • bun
  • hot dog
  • condiments

Steps to make

  1. gather materials
  2. cook hot dog on grill until slightly brown
  3. butter bun
  4. cook bun on grill for 15-30s until golden in center
  5. remove hot dog and bun from grill
  6. place hot dog in bun
  7. add condiments
  8. serve with bread and wine
