Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a pasta bake dish consisting of beef, pasta, cheese and marinara layered and baked in a sheet pan

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • beef
  • marinara
  • cheese

Steps to make

  1. prepare a large baking dish
  2. cover dish in oil
  3. add a layer of pasta
  4. add a layer of cheese
  5. add a layer of beef
  6. repeat
  7. toss cheese on top
  8. preheat oven to 375
  9. bake for an hour
  10. serve with bread and wine
